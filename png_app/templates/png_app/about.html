About Jesus Joenas News Network

Welcome to JJNN — where your news experience is as unique as you are.

This project is part of an academic journey in machine learning and software development. The aim is to create a personalised news platform that learns from your preferences and recommends articles that matter to you.

Behind the scenes, a recommendation engine powered by natural language processing (NLP) carefully selects articles tailored to your interests.

Rather than giving you the same news as everyone else, Jesus Joenas News Network (JJNN) crafts a reading list just for you — because your time (and curiosity) deserve better.

Thanks for visiting — and happy reading!
